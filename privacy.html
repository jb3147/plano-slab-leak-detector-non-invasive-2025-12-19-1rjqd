You may provide information directly to us when you:

  • Use any contact or inquiry forms hosted on this site or linked services.
  • Request information about availability, pricing, or services.

This information may include:

  • Name and contact details (such as email address and phone number).

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website may use cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage or pixels) to support basic functionality, improve performance, and understand how visitors interact with the site.

5.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They allow websites to remember certain information, such as your preferences or whether you have visited before. Cookies can be “session” cookies (which expire when you close your browser) or “persistent” cookies (which remain until they expire or you delete them).

5.2 Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies that are necessary for basic site functionality and security.
  • Performance and analytics cookies that help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve layout, content, and loading times.

5.3 Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking certain types of cookies or deleting existing ones. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect the functionality or performance of this website.

For more detailed guidance on managing cookies, consult your browser’s help documentation or visit resources such as aboutcookies.org (where available).
